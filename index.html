About Me

I am a Ph.D. candidate in Strategy & Organization at emlyon business school, and a member of the Strategy, Organization, and Management (STORM) Research Center. I hold an M.Phil. in Management from emlyon business school, and an MBA in Strategy. With a foundational background in engineering, my research is centered around the intersection of technology, innovation, and organizational strategy.

My specific interests include emergent technologies, the dynamics of innovation, social networks, the geography and diffusion of innovation, and understanding the broader impact of organizations on their communities.
